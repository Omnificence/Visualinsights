About Us
Gravity Consulting has always been about helping our clients see and connect the dots, by turning complex data into intuitive, meaningful visuals. We are industry and domain-agnostic problem solvers. We’ve helped Fortune 500 companies implement strategic initiatives just as easily as we’ve helped Australian Government departments visually map policy dependencies.
Visual Insights is Gravity’s endeavour to take this service to the next level through an online platform. We understand organizational problems and the business implications brought on by the glut of data, and strongly believe visuals can help solve many of these problems as well as communicate insights better.
